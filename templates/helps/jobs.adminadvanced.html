Admin Job Advanced

Here you can edit advanced properities of SYS-Admin job.

Start Backup Time - you can change when a job will be executed. It is running every day.

Job enabled - you can disable (off) or enable again (on) the SYS-Admin job execution.

Disabling a SYS-Admin job is not a good idea. You should consult this with Inteos support unless you know what you are doing.
