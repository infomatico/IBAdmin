New Files Job

Here you can define a new job for files backup.

  • Job name - name of your new job. It has to be unique in your system.
  • Description - any description of your job.
  • Backup Retention - a time how long job will be preserved after backup.
  • Backup Storage - where your job will stored.
  • Backup Client - where your job will executed.
  • Files to Backup - what files/folders you need to backup.
  • Exclude from Backup - what files/folders you want to exclude from backup.
  • Backup Schedule - defines a backup cycle.
  • Start Backup Time - when your job should start.
  • Backup Repeat - how many times your backup will run during a day.
  • Backup Level - level of your backup job.
  • Days of Week - what level is required on any day of the week.
  • Days of Month - what level is required on any day of the month.

Everyday - forces a selected level on every day of the week or month.
