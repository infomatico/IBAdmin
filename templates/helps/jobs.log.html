Job Log

Here you can view all details regarding finished JobId.

Termination Status for the Job is one of: Success, Error, Warning, Cancel or Incomplete.

You can Restore , Edit , Delete or Comment this JobId. Some operations are unavailable for particular job types, i.e. for Restore job the Restore operation.

If Job is unsuccessful then you can Restart it.

Use to view a Statistics of the Job or go stright to the Job Info page with .

Job Log - current log from execution of this JobId.

Errors in Job log are highlighted for better view.

Data Location - information about storage location of this JobId.

Files - information about files saved with JobId.

Restored Files - information about files restored with Restore Job.
