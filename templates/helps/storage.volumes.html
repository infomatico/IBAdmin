Volumes List

Here you can view a list of all your archive Volumes. You can verify a Retention, Media Type, Size and Status of your Volumes. You can manage eny Volume also.

You can always refresh the view using Refresh button -

Display detailed information about any selected Volume.

Close - change status to Used - a selected Volume.

Open - change status to Append - a selected Volume.

Recycle - change status to Purged - a selected Volume.

Delete selected Volume.

You can search or sort the list to find or check what you are looking for.

Deleteing a volume from application does not delete it from Storage, you need to delete it manually.
